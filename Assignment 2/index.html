Contemporary Cowboy Chic

The "Yeehaw Agenda" is a popular culture trend, movement, and aesthetic exploding 2018-2019. Lead by a new generation of Black artists and celebrities, the Yeehaw Agenda drastically reimagined cowboy and western culture in dress, music, and attitude for the Internet Age.

This aesthetic involves denim, wide legged jeans, colorful bandanas, bedazzled belt buckles, cowhide, cow print, gallon hats, boots, and chaps.
