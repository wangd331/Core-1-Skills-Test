Hi there! My name is Deanna and I have severe high-frequency hearing loss.

In speech, it is difficult for me to hear soft sounds and consonants like c, ch, s, sh, ff.

As a child, playing the game Telephone was a clumsy and frustrating experience. I had no idea that my complete lack of skill in the game was due to my impairment! My hearing loss to high-frequencies made it impossible to decipher whispers.

This site is a comical take on a part of my everyday experience. I hope you enjoy!
